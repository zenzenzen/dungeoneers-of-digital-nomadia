# Locations of Digital Nomadia

## The Coworking Space of Eternal Silence
**Description**: A pristine, white-walled sanctuary of productivity. The air is conditioned to a frigid 18°C. The only sounds are the aggressive clacking of mechanical keyboards and the hushed whispers of "synergy."
**Hazards**:
-   **The Glare**: If you make a noise above 10 decibels, roll a DC 15 Charisma save or take 1d6 Psychic damage from the collective judgment of the room.
-   **The Unstable WiFi**: Every hour, roll a d20. On a 1, the internet goes down, causing the **Panic** condition.
**Loot**:
-   Abandoned chargers.
-   Business cards of people you'll never email.
-   Free (but terrible) coffee.

## The Overpriced Avocado Toast Cafe
**Description**: A trendy cafe with exposed brick walls and succulents on every table. The menu is on a QR code that doesn't load.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Wait**: You must wait 45 minutes for a table. Make a Constitution save (DC 12) or gain one level of **Exhaustion**.
-   **The Bill**: When paying, make a Wisdom save (DC 14). On a failure, you tip 30% out of guilt.
**Loot**:
-   Artisanal pastries (restores 1d4 Chill).
-   WiFi password written on a napkin (Key Item).

## The Airbnb That Looked Better In Photos
**Description**: The listing promised "Cozy, rustic charm in the heart of the city." In reality, it's a damp basement next to a construction site.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Construction Noise**: Starts at 6 AM. Prevents Long Rests unless you have **Noise Cancelling Headphones**.
-   **The Mystery Smell**: DC 13 Constitution save every hour or become **Nauseated**.
**Loot**:
-   One (1) towel.
-   A half-used bottle of shampoo.
-   A passive-aggressive note from the host.

## The Gentrified Neighborhood
**Description**: Once a vibrant cultural hub, now a row of identical glass condos and specialty dog bakeries.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Rent Hike**: Every turn, you lose 1d10 Gold/Bitcoin.
-   **The HOA**: A patrol of Karens challenges your presence. DC 14 Deception check to prove you belong.
**Loot**:
-   Organic dog treats.
-   A "Live Laugh Love" sign.

## The Craft Brewery
**Description**: A warehouse with concrete floors and picnic tables. Everyone is drinking IPAs with IBUs higher than their credit scores.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Snobbery**: If you order a lager, take 1d8 Psychic damage.
-   **The Alcohol Content**: Drinks are 9% ABV. Con save DC 15 or become **Intoxicated**.
**Loot**:
-   Growler of "Hoppy McHopface".
-   Beard oil.

## The Vintage Clothing Store
**Description**: Smells like mothballs and desperation. Clothes from the 90s are sold for 10x their original price.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Dust**: DC 12 Con save or start sneezing (Disadvantage on Stealth).
-   **The Fit**: Nothing actually fits. DC 14 Dex check to put on a pair of jeans.
**Loot**:
-   A band t-shirt of a band you don't know.
-   Oversized denim jacket (+1 Armor).

## The Farmer's Market
**Description**: A Sunday morning ritual. Fresh produce, overpriced honey, and aggressive buskers.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Crowd**: Difficult Terrain.
-   **The Free Samples**: DC 13 Wis save or spend all your money on artisanal cheese.
**Loot**:
-   Fresh sourdough bread.
-   Local honey (cures Allergies).

## The Music Festival
**Description**: A dusty field filled with glitter, sweat, and bass.
**Hazards**:
-   **Dehydration**: Must drink water every hour or take 1d6 damage.
-   **The Port-a-Potty**: DC 18 Con save to enter.
**Loot**:
-   Glow sticks.
-   Lost sunglasses.

## The Tech Conference
**Description**: A massive convention center filled with booths, swag, and desperate networking.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Pitch**: Random NPCs will try to pitch you their startup. DC 14 Wis save to escape.
-   **The Swag Bag**: Encumbrance. You are carrying 50lbs of useless pens and stress balls.
**Loot**:
-   High-quality t-shirt (Corporate branding).
-   Stickers.

## The Startup Incubator
**Description**: An open-plan office with bean bags, ping pong tables, and a palpable sense of panic.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Pivot**: The layout of the dungeon changes every 1d4 rounds.
-   **The Burnout**: Spending more than 1 hour here causes 1 level of Exhaustion.
**Loot**:
-   Equity (worthless).
-   Ping pong paddle (+1 Club).

## The Rooftop Bar
**Description**: The place to be seen. Great views, terrible cocktails.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Dress Code**: If you aren't wearing "Smart Casual", you cannot enter.
-   **The Selfie**: You must take a selfie every 10 minutes or lose 1d4 Chill.
**Loot**:
-   Overpriced cocktail (Potion of Confidence).
-   Phone number of a promoter.

## The Underground Rave
**Description**: A dark basement with flashing lights and techno music.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Bass**: 1d4 Sonic damage per round.
-   **The Sweat**: Disadvantage on Grapple checks due to slipperiness.
**Loot**:
-   Mysterious pills (Do not eat).
-   Cool sunglasses.

# BANGKOK LOCATIONS

## Khao San Road
**Description**: The legendary backpacker strip. Neon signs, bucket drinks, fake IDs, and the smell of pad thai mixed with desperation. Every fifth person is trying to sell you something.
**Hazards**:
-   **Bucket Roulette**: CON save DC 14 or become Poisoned. On a 1, gain Blackout condition.
-   **Aggressive Tailors**: CHA save DC 12 every 30 feet or be Grappled into a fitting.
-   **The Noise**: Disadvantage on all WIS checks from sensory overload.
**Loot**:
-   Fisherman pants (+1 CR, -2 CHA with locals).
-   Fake student ID (-50% on entry fees, INT check DC 15 to spot).
-   Chang singlet (armor, -2 CHA, +2 CON saves vs alcohol).

## Sukhumvit Soi 11
**Description**: Expat central. International restaurants, rooftop bars, and the perpetual question of "what do you do here?"
**Hazards**:
-   **The Networking Trap**: Every conversation requires a CHA save DC 13 or you're stuck hearing about someone's startup.
-   **Price Inflation**: All items cost 3x standard price.
-   **The Expat Interrogation**: Must answer "How long have you been here?" and "What do you do?" within 2 rounds.
**Loot**:
-   Business cards (worthless currency).
-   Craft cocktail (restores 1d6 Chill, costs 5 GP).
-   Coworking space day pass.

## Chatuchak Weekend Market
**Description**: 15,000 stalls of organized chaos. You will get lost. You will buy things you don't need. The AC sections are oases.
**Hazards**:
-   **Maze Navigation**: WIS save DC 14 to find exit. On failure, lose 1d4 hours.
-   **Impulse Buying**: WIS save DC 12 or spend 2d10 GP on random items.
-   **Heat Exhaustion**: CON save DC 13 every hour or gain Exhaustion level.
**Loot**:
-   Vintage finds (roll on random armor table).
-   Coconut ice cream (removes 1 Exhaustion level).
-   That thing you definitely needed.

## Thonglor
**Description**: Where Bangkok's wealthy and long-term expats pretend they're not in Thailand. Japanese restaurants, fancy coffee, and uncomfortable fashion.
**Hazards**:
-   **Social Hierarchy Check**: CHA check DC 14 to enter premium establishments.
-   **The Judgment**: -2 to all CHA checks if wearing tourist clothing.
-   **Hipster Density**: INT save DC 12 or feel inadequate about your coffee knowledge.
**Loot**:
-   Japanese curry (restores 2d6 Chill).
-   Overpriced vintage clothing (+2 CR, costs 10 GP).
-   Craft coffee (advantage on INT checks for 1 hour).

## Soi Cowboy
**Description**: Neon-lit bar street. A monument to complicated feelings and poor decisions. Surprisingly small.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Approach**: CHA saves DC 11 constantly or be engaged in conversation.
-   **Reputation Risk**: WIS save DC 14 or do something you regret (2d6 reputation damage).
-   **The Bill**: INT check DC 16 to verify correct amount.
**Loot**:
-   Stories you won't tell.
-   Lighter with bar logo.
-   Questionable memories.

## MBK Center
**Description**: Seven floors of phones, phone cases, phone accessories, and the occasional knockoff. Air-conditioned heaven.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Haggle**: Persuasion contest for every purchase. Lose = pay 2x price.
-   **Fake Goods Risk**: INT check DC 14 to spot fakes. Failure = item breaks in 1d4 days.
-   **Food Court Confusion**: 50+ options, WIS save DC 12 or be Paralyzed with indecision.
**Loot**:
-   Phone repair (removes tech damage conditions).
-   Knockoff headphones (50% chance of +1, 50% chance of 0).
-   Food court meal (1d4+1 Chill, 20 CP).

## Wat Pho
**Description**: Temple of the Reclining Buddha. A place of genuine peace if you can ignore the tour groups.
**Hazards**:
-   **Tour Group Collision**: DEX save DC 12 or be swept into a tour.
-   **The Dress Code**: Must cover shoulders and knees or rent sarong (1 GP, -1 CHA).
-   **Sacred Silence**: Any noise above whisper triggers DC 15 CHA save vs Shame.
**Loot**:
-   Traditional Thai massage (removes all Exhaustion, 2 GP).
-   Blessed amulet (+1 WIS saves).
-   Inner peace (advantage on WIS saves for 24 hours).

## Victory Monument Minivan Station
**Description**: Organized chaos of minivans going everywhere. The gateway to escape Bangkok.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Squeeze**: CON save DC 13 or gain Claustrophobic condition (Disadvantage for trip).
-   **Driver's Playlist**: WIS save DC 11 or 2d4 sonic damage from Thai pop.
-   **The Mystery Stop**: 25% chance van stops somewhere random for 1d4 hours.
**Loot**:
-   Cheap transportation (1-5 GP depending on distance).
-   Snacks from 7-Eleven.
-   Near-death experience stories.

## Yaowarat (Chinatown)
**Description**: Bangkok's Chinatown. Gold shops, street food heaven, and the best soup you'll never find again.
**Hazards**:
-   **Food Coma**: After eating, CON save DC 12 or speed halved for 1 hour.
-   **Gold Shop Temptation**: WIS save DC 14 or spend 3d20 GP on jewelry.
-   **The Crowd**: Difficult terrain during dinner hours.
**Loot**:
-   Legendary street food (restores 3d6 Chill).
-   Gold chain (+2 CHA, costs 50 GP).
-   Shark fin soup (ethically questionable, +2 CON).

## Onsen Coworking Space (Ekkamai)
**Description**: Where the serious nomads work. Good wifi, good coffee, too much networking.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Pitch**: Every hour, CHA save DC 13 or hear about someone's course/product.
-   **Comparisonitis**: WIS save DC 14 or take 1d6 psychic damage from others' success.
-   **The Slack Ping**: Cannot achieve Deep Focus without Noise Cancelling Headphones.
**Loot**:
-   Solid wifi (no random disconnects).
-   Networking contacts (50% useful, 50% crypto bros).
-   Overpriced but good coffee.

# TOKYO LOCATIONS

## Shibuya Crossing
**Description**: The world's busiest pedestrian crossing. A river of humanity flowing in organized chaos.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Crush**: DEX save DC 14 during peak hours or take 1d4 bludgeoning from crowds.
-   **Tourist Photo Freeze**: WIS save DC 12 or waste action taking photos.
-   **Sensory Overload**: First time visitors: WIS save DC 14 or Stunned for 1 round.
**Loot**:
-   Perfect crossing photo (+2 CHA on social media).
-   Hachiko stories (advantage on CHA checks with dog lovers).
-   Starbucks with view (1d4 Chill, costs 4x normal).

## Shinjuku Station
**Description**: The world's busiest train station. 200+ exits. You will get lost. Everyone does.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Maze**: INT check DC 16 to find correct exit first try. DC 14 second try.
-   **Rush Hour**: DEX save DC 15 or be pushed into wrong train. CON save DC 14 to breathe.
-   **The Guilt**: Miss your train = 1d4 psychic damage from Japanese disappointment aura.
**Loot**:
-   Ekiben (train bento, restores 1d8 Chill).
-   Suica card (essential transport item).
-   Platform milk (mysterious healing properties, 1d4 Chill).

## Akihabara
**Description**: Electric Town. Anime, manga, electronics, and maid cafes. Sensory assault for weebs.
**Hazards**:
-   **Maid Cafe Trap**: CHA save DC 13 or pay 30 GP for mediocre coffee with "moe moe kyun."
-   **Gacha Addiction**: WIS save DC 14 per machine or spend 1d10 GP.
-   **The Cringe**: Non-weebs take 1d4 psychic damage per hour from secondhand embarrassment.
**Loot**:
-   Rare figurines (+3 CHA with weebs, -3 CHA with everyone else).
-   Retro games (various effects).
-   Questionable purchases in brown bags.

## Golden Gai
**Description**: 200+ tiny bars in narrow alleys. Each seats 6-8 people. Each has its own rules and vibe.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Cover Charge**: Random 500-2000 yen cover. WIS save DC 12 to notice before sitting.
-   **The Rejection**: Some bars are regulars only. CHA check DC 16 to enter.
-   **The Intimacy**: Must make conversation. No hiding. Social combat imminent.
**Loot**:
-   Genuine connections (rare).
-   Perfect whisky highball (restores 1d6 Chill).
-   Stories from Mama-san (quest hooks).

## Roppongi
**Description**: Tokyo's foreigner party district. Clubs, bars, and the inevitable regret.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Tout**: Every 10 feet, CHA save DC 11 or be engaged by club promoters.
-   **The Bill**: INT check DC 18 at clubs to avoid scam charges.
-   **Morning Train of Shame**: CON save DC 14 or Exhaustion level the next day.
**Loot**:
-   Club stamp collection (worthless).
-   Nigerian promoter's card.
-   Memories you question.

## Tsukiji Outer Market
**Description**: The remaining market after the inner market moved. Still amazing for food and kitchen goods.
**Hazards**:
-   **Early Morning Required**: Must arrive before 9 AM or best items gone. CON save DC 13 if waking early.
-   **Impulse Knife Purchase**: WIS save DC 14 or buy 50+ GP knife you don't need.
-   **Standing Sushi Pressure**: Must eat quickly. CHA save DC 12 or feel rushed.
**Loot**:
-   Fresh sushi (restores 2d8 Chill).
-   Quality kitchen knife (+2 INT for cooking).
-   Tamagoyaki (Japanese omelet, 1d4 Chill).

## Harajuku/Takeshita Street
**Description**: Youth fashion epicenter. Crepes, costume shops, and more style per square foot than anywhere on earth.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Crowd**: Weekend = Difficult terrain + DEX save DC 13 to move.
-   **Fashion Pressure**: WIS save DC 14 or spend 2d20 GP on clothes you'll never wear at home.
-   **Crepe Queue**: Must wait 1d4 × 10 minutes for famous crepe.
**Loot**:
-   Harajuku fashion (various armor, see Asian Fashion armors).
-   Rainbow crepe (restores 1d6 Chill, highly Instagrammable).
-   Costume accessories (+2 to specific Performance checks).

## Don Quijote (Donki)
**Description**: Discount store meets fever dream. 24 hours of compressed aisles and the Donki song on loop.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Song**: WIS save DC 13 per hour or the "Don Don Don Donki" song lives in your head for 1d4 days.
-   **Impulse Maze**: Store is designed to confuse. WIS save DC 14 or buy random item.
-   **Time Dilation**: Time moves strangely. What feels like 30 minutes is actually 2 hours.
**Loot**:
-   Tax-free electronics.
-   Weird Kit Kats (various minor effects).
-   That thing you forgot you needed.

## Senso-ji Temple (Asakusa)
**Description**: Tokyo's oldest temple. A moment of tradition among the modern chaos.
**Hazards**:
-   **Fortune Risk**: Drawing omikuji (fortune). Bad fortune = -1 to next roll until you tie it to the rack.
-   **Nakamise Dori Shopping**: WIS save DC 12 to resist souvenirs.
-   **Photo Kimono Rental**: CHA save DC 14 or rent kimono for photos (5 GP).
**Loot**:
-   Omamori charm (+1 to specific save, varies).
-   Ningyo-yaki (doll cakes, 1d4 Chill).
-   Respect for tradition (advantage on CHA with traditional Japanese NPCs).

## Hub Izakaya (Any Location)
**Description**: The gaijin-friendly izakaya chain. Where foreigners gather when they want "Japanese" experience with English menus.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Networking**: 100% chance of meeting other expats. CHA save DC 11 or conversation.
-   **Hub Pricing**: All drinks 2x normal izakaya price.
-   **The Cringe**: WIS save DC 14 for locals observing the gaijin gathering.
**Loot**:
-   Safe menu choices.
-   Expat contacts (various usefulness).
-   Comfortable mediocrity.

# SHANGHAI LOCATIONS

## The Bund
**Description**: Shanghai's famous waterfront. Colonial architecture on one side, Pudong's alien skyline on the other.
**Hazards**:
-   **Tourist Photo Requests**: Every 10 minutes, CHA save DC 11 or be asked for photo.
-   **The Selfie Stick Forest**: DEX save DC 12 or take 1d4 bludgeoning from selfie sticks.
-   **Scam Artists**: WIS save DC 14 to avoid tea ceremony/art gallery scams.
**Loot**:
-   Iconic photos (+2 CHA on social media).
-   Fake luxury goods (CHA +2 until inspected).
-   Overpriced Starbucks with view.

## French Concession
**Description**: Tree-lined streets, boutique shops, and where expats pretend they're in Paris. Best coffee in Shanghai.
**Hazards**:
-   **Brunch Trap**: WIS save DC 13 or spend 50+ GP on overpriced Western food.
-   **Hipster Density**: Must pass CHA DC 14 vibe check for some establishments.
-   **The Nostalgia**: Long-term expats trapped here take 1d4 psychic damage from "how it used to be."
**Loot**:
-   Excellent coffee (+2 INT for 1 hour).
-   Boutique clothing (+2 CR, 15+ GP).
-   Expat social calendar.

## Jing'an Temple Area
**Description**: Ancient temple surrounded by luxury malls. Peak Shanghai juxtaposition.
**Hazards**:
-   **Mall Temptation**: WIS save DC 15 to resist luxury brands. Failure = 5d20 GP.
-   **Spiritual Whiplash**: Going from Gucci to Buddhist temple: WIS save DC 12 or Confused.
-   **The Prices**: Everything here costs 3x normal.
**Loot**:
-   Designer goods (+3 CR, costs fortune).
-   Temple blessings (+1 WIS saves for 24 hours).
-   Perfect juxtaposition photos.

## Pudong Business District
**Description**: The financial heart. Glass towers, business suits, and the feeling that money is being made somewhere above you.
**Hazards**:
-   **Imposter Syndrome**: If not wearing business attire, -2 to all CHA checks.
-   **The Flex**: Constant exposure to wealth. WIS save DC 14 or take 1d6 psychic damage.
-   **Altitude Sickness**: In high towers, CON save DC 12 or -1 to checks from ear pressure.
**Loot**:
-   Networking with actual money.
-   Observatory views (impressive for dates, +2 CHA).
-   Expensive cocktails.

## Tianzifang
**Description**: Artsy alley complex in the French Concession. Boutiques, cafes, and getting lost in identical alleys.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Maze**: WIS save DC 13 to find specific shop again. Nearly impossible.
-   **Artisanal Markup**: All prices 2x. No haggling (this isn't that kind of market).
-   **The Instagram Crowds**: Difficult terrain on weekends.
**Loot**:
-   Handmade jewelry and crafts.
-   Unique souvenirs (+1 CHA when telling story).
-   Actual art (??d?? GP, you don't really know).

## Fake Market (AP Plaza)
**Description**: Underground labyrinth of counterfeit everything. A rite of passage for Shanghai expats.
**Hazards**:
-   **Aggressive Vendors**: Grapple attempts every stall. STR or DEX save DC 12 to keep moving.
-   **The Haggle Battle**: Persuasion contest. Starting price is 10x final price. Must counter-offer.
-   **Quality Roulette**: Goods range from decent to falling apart. INT check DC 14 to assess.
**Loot**:
-   Fake watches, bags, clothes (various quality).
-   "This is best quality" (narrator: it wasn't).
-   Haggling experience (+2 Persuasion for commerce).

## People's Square Marriage Market
**Description**: Where parents advertise their children for marriage on weekends. Umbrellas with stats.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Interrogation**: If approaching, intense questioning about salary, property, and hukou.
-   **Existential Crisis**: Seeing yourself reduced to stats: WIS save DC 15 or 2d6 psychic damage.
-   **Cultural Whiplash**: WIS save DC 14 to process what you're seeing.
**Loot**:
-   Understanding of Chinese family pressure.
-   Stories to tell.
-   Perspective on your own dating life.

## Xintiandi
**Description**: Restored shikumen (old Shanghai houses) turned into expensive restaurants and bars. Where deals happen.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Price Tag**: Everything costs 5x normal Shanghai prices.
-   **Business Dress Code**: Without proper attire, -3 to CHA checks.
-   **Baijiu Toast Trap**: CON save DC 16 or be pressured into drinking baijiu with businessmen.
**Loot**:
-   High-level contacts.
-   Expensive but good food.
-   Stories of excess.

## WeWork Jing'an
**Description**: Shanghai's expat coworking hub. Where startups come to make it or fake it in China.
**Hazards**:
-   **VPN Roulette**: 30% chance VPN doesn't work. INT check DC 14 to fix.
-   **The Pivot Story**: Every hour, must listen to someone explain their China strategy.
-   **Coffee Machine Line**: 1d10 minute wait during peak hours.
**Loot**:
-   Actual work done (rare).
-   Business connections.
-   Free beer Fridays.

## Waibaidu Bridge Night Market
**Description**: Night food market with views of the Bund. Peak Shanghai food experience.
**Hazards**:
-   **Decision Paralysis**: So many options. WIS save DC 14 or waste 30 minutes deciding.
-   **The Queue**: Best stalls have 30+ minute waits.
-   **Spice Challenge**: Some foods are deceptively spicy. CON save DC 15 or fire damage.
**Loot**:
-   Xiaolongbao (soup dumplings, 2d6 Chill).
-   Lamb skewers (1d6 Chill).
-   Local beer (1d4 Chill, cheap).

# SEOUL LOCATIONS

## Hongdae
**Description**: University area. Clubs, street performers, and the sense that everyone is younger and cooler than you.
**Hazards**:
-   **Age Check**: If over 30, -2 to all CHA checks. They can sense it.
-   **K-Pop Everywhere**: WIS save DC 12 or find yourself humming songs you don't know.
-   **Club Hopping**: Each club requires CON save DC 13 to continue.
**Loot**:
-   Indie music discoveries.
-   Soju in parks (1d6 Chill, illegal but nobody cares).
-   Youth by proximity.

## Gangnam
**Description**: The song was about satire. This is where money lives in Seoul. Plastic surgery and luxury.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Gaze**: Everyone is beautiful. Constant passive CHA comparison.
-   **Price Reality**: Everything costs 3x normal Seoul prices.
-   **Plastic Surgery Ads**: Every surface. WIS save DC 14 or consider "minor work."
**Loot**:
-   Designer goods.
-   High-end coffee.
-   Feeling inadequate.

## Myeongdong
**Description**: Shopping district. Skincare shops every 3 feet. You will leave with 10 sheet masks minimum.
**Hazards**:
-   **Sheet Mask Assault**: Sales people physically put masks on your hand. CHA save DC 11 to refuse.
-   **Skincare Rabbit Hole**: WIS save DC 15 or spend 3d10 GP on products you don't understand.
-   **The Crowds**: Always difficult terrain.
**Loot**:
-   K-beauty products (various CHA bonuses).
-   Street food (tteokbokki, etc.).
-   More sheet masks than you need.

## Itaewon
**Description**: International district. Where foreigners feel at home and Koreans go to feel foreign.
**Hazards**:
-   **Expat Bubble Risk**: WIS save DC 14 or forget you're in Korea.
-   **Weekend Chaos**: Friday/Saturday nights: CON save DC 15 or regret.
-   **The Hill**: STR save DC 12 or Exhaustion from geography.
**Loot**:
-   International food (any cuisine).
-   Vintage shops (Haebangchon).
-   Community connections.

## Bukchon Hanok Village
**Description**: Traditional Korean houses. Instagram heaven. Residents increasingly annoyed by tourists.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Silence**: Must be quiet. Fail DC 13 WIS check = angry residents = -2 CHA.
-   **Hanbok Photo Rental**: CHA save DC 14 or rent hanbok for photos (3 GP).
-   **Getting Lost**: Alleys all look the same. WIS save DC 14 to find exit.
**Loot**:
-   Traditional tea experience (removes 1 stress condition).
-   Cultural photos.
-   Appreciation for preservation.

## Gwangjang Market
**Description**: Traditional market. Best bindaetteok (mung bean pancakes) in Seoul. Food stall paradise.
**Hazards**:
-   **Food Coma**: After eating, CON save DC 14 or speed halved.
-   **Soju Pressure**: Grandmas will offer soju. CHA save DC 12 to politely refuse.
-   **Stall Selection**: 100+ options. WIS save DC 14 or wander for 1 hour.
**Loot**:
-   Bindaetteok (2d6 Chill).
-   Yukhoe (beef tartare, +2 CON for 1 hour).
-   Mayak gimbap (addictive, WIS save DC 13 to stop eating).

## Jamsil Lotte World
**Description**: Indoor theme park. K-pop concerts. The Mall of America but make it Korean.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Lines**: Peak times = 2+ hour waits. CON save DC 14 to endure.
-   **Couples Everywhere**: If single, WIS save DC 12 or 1d4 psychic damage.
-   **Spending Spiral**: So many things to buy. WIS save DC 13 every hour.
**Loot**:
-   Memories (varying quality).
-   Character merchandise.
-   Photos with K-pop standees.

## Noryangjin Fish Market
**Description**: Massive seafood market. Pick your fish, they cook it upstairs. 3 AM sashimi is a lifestyle.
**Hazards**:
-   **Negotiation Required**: Must haggle or pay 2x price.
-   **The Freshness**: Creatures are very alive. WIS save DC 12 if squeamish.
-   **Soju Integration**: Dealers assume you're drinking. CON save DC 13 to pace yourself.
**Loot**:
-   Fresh sashimi (3d6 Chill).
-   Live octopus (CON save DC 14 to eat, +3 CON if successful).
-   Stories.

## Dongdaemun Design Plaza
**Description**: Zaha Hadid's alien building. Fashion market. 24-hour shopping for night owls.
**Hazards**:
-   **Time Distortion**: Open until 5 AM. Easy to lose track of time.
-   **Wholesale Confusion**: Prices may require minimum quantities.
-   **Fashion Overload**: So many options. WIS save DC 15 or analysis paralysis.
**Loot**:
-   Cheap fashion (various armor).
-   K-fashion trends.
-   Sleep deprivation.

## Pojangmacha (Street Tent Bars)
**Description**: Orange tent bars serving soju and anju (drinking food). Peak Korean drinking culture.
**Hazards**:
-   **The Heart-to-Heart**: Soju leads to deep conversations. WIS save DC 14 or share secrets.
-   **The Bill Confusion**: Who pays? It's complicated. CHA check to navigate.
-   **Morning Reality**: CON save DC 16 or Exhaustion level 2 next day.
**Loot**:
-   Genuine Korean experience.
-   New friends (maybe).
-   Fried chicken (restores 2d6 Chill).
-   Emotional catharsis.

## COEX Mall
**Description**: Underground maze. The largest underground shopping mall in Asia. You will get lost.
**Hazards**:
-   **Navigation Nightmare**: INT check DC 17 to find specific store first try.
-   **The Starfield Library**: WIS save DC 12 or spend 2 hours taking photos of books.
-   **Aquarium Distraction**: Must visit aquarium. It's mandatory.
**Loot**:
