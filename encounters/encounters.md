# Encounters in Digital Nomadia

## The Tinder Date from Hell
**Enemy**: **Delusional Date** (CR 3)
**Setting**: A dimly lit bar or coffee shop.
**Description**: You matched with them 2 hours ago. They look nothing like their photos and have already mentioned their ex 5 times.
**Tactics**:
-   **Trauma Dump**: (Action) Target must make a DC 14 Wisdom save or take 2d6 Psychic damage and be **Stunned** by the oversharing.
-   **Split the Bill**: (Reaction) When the check comes, they vanish to the bathroom. You must pay or make a DC 16 Intimidation check.
**Loot**:
-   Leftover fries.
-   A blocked number.

## The Mansplaining Tech Bro
**Enemy**: **Tech Bro** (CR 2)
**Setting**: Coworking space or tech conference.
**Description**: Wearing a Patagonia vest and holding a craft beer. He assumes you don't know what "The Cloud" is.
**Tactics**:
-   **Actually...**: (Reaction) When you make an Intelligence check, he interrupts. Reroll with Disadvantage.
-   **Whiteboard**: (Action) He draws a diagram. Everyone in 30ft must make a DC 13 Con save or fall asleep.
**Loot**:
-   Patagonia Vest (+1 Armor).
-   Stickers for a failed startup.

## The Karen in the Wild
**Enemy**: **Karen** (CR 5)
**Setting**: Retail store or HOA meeting.
**Description**: She wants to speak to the manager. Now.
**Tactics**:
-   **Summon Manager**: (Action) Summons a **Manager** (CR 1) to fight for her.
-   **Screech**: (Action) 3d6 Sonic damage in a 15ft cone.
**Loot**:
-   Expired coupon.
-   Oversized sunglasses.

## The MLM Recruiter
**Enemy**: **Boss Babe** (CR 3)
**Setting**: Facebook Messenger or a high school reunion.
**Description**: "Hey hun! Long time no see! I have an amazing business opportunity for you."
**Tactics**:
-   **Pyramid Scheme**: (Action) Grapples you. You take 1d6 Gold damage per turn until you escape.
-   **Toxic Positivity**: (Passive) Immune to negative emotions (Psychic damage).
**Loot**:
-   Box of unsold essential oils.
-   Pink Cadillac key (fake).

## The Crypto Scammer
**Enemy**: **Rug Puller** (CR 4)
**Setting**: Twitter DMs or a Telegram group.
**Description**: Profile picture is an ape. Promises 1000x returns.
**Tactics**:
-   **Pump and Dump**: (Action) Raises your hopes (Chill), then crashes them. Target loses 50% of current Chill.
-   **FUD**: (Action) Creates an aura of fear. DC 14 Wis save or be Frightened.
**Loot**:
-   USB drive with 0.0001 BTC.
-   Paper hands.

## The Influencer in the Wild
**Enemy**: **TikToker** (CR 2)
**Setting**: A scenic viewpoint or busy street.
**Description**: Dancing in front of a ring light, blocking traffic.
**Tactics**:
-   **Main Character Energy**: (Passive) Difficult Terrain for everyone else within 10ft.
-   **Collaboration Request**: (Action) Asks for free stuff. DC 12 Cha save or you give them an item.
**Loot**:
-   Ring light.
-   Portable charger.

## The Aggressive Busker
**Enemy**: **Soundcloud Rapper** (CR 1)
**Setting**: Subway station or city square.
**Description**: Handing out mixtapes that nobody wants.
**Tactics**:
-   **Freestyle**: (Action) 1d4 Sonic damage. On a 1, he hurts himself.
-   **Guilt Trip**: (Action) DC 10 Cha save or give him 1 Gold.
**Loot**:
-   Mixtape (Fire hazard).
-   Loose change.

## The Lost Tourist
**Enemy**: **Boomer Tourist** (CR 1/2)
**Setting**: Standing in the middle of the bike lane looking at a paper map.
**Description**: Wearing socks with sandals and a fanny pack.
**Tactics**:
-   **Obstruction**: (Passive) Blocks movement.
-   **Loud Question**: (Action) Asks "DO YOU SPEAK ENGLISH?" at 100 decibels. 1d4 Sonic damage.
**Loot**:
-   Fanny pack.
-   Paper map of the wrong city.
