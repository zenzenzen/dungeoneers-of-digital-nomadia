# Status Afflictions Compendium

> A comprehensive reference of all conditions, buffs, debuffs, and status effects in Dungeoneers of Digital Nomadia. Combat is social, violence is vibes, and your mental state is your health bar.

|-----------|--------|----------|-----------|----------|

## CONDITION INTERACTIONS

### Conditions That Stack
- Exhaustion (levels 1-6)
- Reputation damage (cumulative toward Cancelled)
- Annoyance (multiple sources compound)

### Conditions That Don't Stack (Take Worst)
- Frightened (from multiple sources)
- Charmed (latest charm takes precedence)
- Disadvantage (doesn't stack with itself)

### Conditions That Cancel Each Other
- Charmed vs Frightened (from same source)
- Haste then Exhaustion (automatic after Haste ends)
- Hustle then Burnout (risk after excessive hustle)
